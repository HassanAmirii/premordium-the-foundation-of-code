Welcome Premordium is a curated guide to the essential foundations of computer science and computational thinking. Built for both newcomers and curious minds, this project explores how computers work, how information flows through machines, and the timeless principles that shape modern technology. Topics include Mathematics for computer science, Data structure & algorithms, and Foundations of Computation every developer and engineer should understand.
